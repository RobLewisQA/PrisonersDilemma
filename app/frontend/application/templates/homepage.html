Welcome to the Prisoners' Dilemma Training Camp and Dojo



The Prisoners' Dilemma is a game used to understand how we should make decisions under specific circumstances.

It is modelled on a 2 person/player interaction, where both players move at the same time and where each player only has 2 options - cooperate or defect


The scoring works as follows:


if both players cooperate, both score 4 points

if both players defect, both score 2 points

if one player cooperates while the other defects, the cooperator scores 0 points and the defector scores 8 points
